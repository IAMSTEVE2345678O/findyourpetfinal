# PetSaver 3000 — Open Questions

Answer these to fill in the missing details for the PRD.

## Lost Pet Listings

1. What information does an owner fill in when posting a lost pet? (e.g. pet name, breed, color, age, last seen date, last seen location, photo, owner contact info — anything else?) - They can either insert a photo of their dog, insert their dog breed, or color
2. Can anyone post a listing, or do they need to create an account first? - They have to create an account in order to use the website
3. How does an owner mark their pet as "found" and close the listing? - They can find the username of the person who reported the lost dog and they can text there in the text portal
4. What happens to the listing after the pet is found — does it disappear, or stay up as a success story? - The case can be marked "solved" by the pet owner, and they can choose to keep the case up or archive it.

## Reward System

5. How does the owner set the reward — do they type in a dollar amount, or pick from preset options? - They type in an amount in dollar amount.
6. How does the reward actually get paid — cash in person, online payment, something else? - The money will be paid in person
7. How does a neighbor prove they found the right pet before collecting the reward? - The person will reach out in the text portal, telling the pet owner they found the pet with a picture as proof, and the pet owner will tell them to meet at a specific place, so they can drop off the pet.
8. What stops someone from faking a find to collect the reward? - An A.I will analyze and scan every detail of the pet and compare to the picture to the person who sent the picture of the founded pet

11. Can neighbors contact the owner directly through the site, or only through the contact info the owner posts? - They can contact the pet owner with the texting portal via phone number

15. How long does the pro have to find the pet before the refund kicks in? - They have 7 days or when they say they can't find the pet
