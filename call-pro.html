■ FAQ
What happens after I book?
Three hunters are assigned within 2 hours. They divide your pet's last-seen neighborhood into sectors and begin searching the same day, reporting back to you each evening.
How do hunters know where to look?
They use the details from your listing — description, photo, last-seen location, and local knowledge — to target the most likely spots and known shelters in the area.
How do I get a refund?
If the search is unsuccessful after 7 days, a refund report is filed automatically and the $50 is returned to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.
Can I book without an open listing?
Yes — you can enter your pet's details manually in the booking form. We recommend posting a listing too, since community sightings often lead to the fastest reunions.
